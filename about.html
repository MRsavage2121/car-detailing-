Our Story

Titan Detailing was founded in 2026 by lifelong car and truck enthusiasts, Jamarion Ferguson and Kameron Terrazas. We started with a simple goal: provide the highest level of detailing possible, restoring vehicles to their showroom shine and protecting them for the long haul.

What We Do

Titan Detailing specializes exclusively in automotive and truck detailing. From daily drivers to lifted trucks, performance cars, and luxury SUVs, we deliver services designed to protect your investment and make your vehicle look flawless. Our services include:

  • Exterior washes and paint decontamination
  • Interior deep cleaning for cars and trucks
  • Paint correction & polishing
  • Ceramic coatings for lasting protection
  • Truck & SUV detailing packages built for larger vehicles

The Titan Standard

We don’t believe in shortcuts. Every detail is performed with precision, using professional-grade equipment and premium products. Whether it’s a compact car or a lifted truck, every vehicle leaves our care looking better than the day it rolled off the lot.
